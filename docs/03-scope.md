# Project Scope

## In Scope

- User management with role-based access control.
- Product management, including activation and deactivation without data loss.
- Inventory control with stock updates and minimum stock alerts.
- Sales registration, confirmation, cancellation, and inventory synchronization.
- Automatic calculation of sales totals and estimated profits.
- Generation of basic sales and product performance reports.
- Simulation of a business period through historical sales and inventory data.

## Out of Scope

- Electronic invoicing or tax compliance (e.g., SAT integration).
- Online payments or payment gateway integrations.
- Customer-facing applications (e-commerce or mobile apps).
- Advanced analytics or predictive models.
- Multi-store or multi-branch management.
- High-availability or distributed system architecture.
