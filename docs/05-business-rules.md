# Business Rules

BR-01: A product must not be sold if the available stock is less than the requested quantity.

BR-02: Product stock must be automatically reduced when a sale is confirmed.

BR-03: If a sale is canceled, the system must restore the product stock to its previous state.

BR-04: Products marked as inactive must not be available for new sales.

BR-05: A sale must include at least one product to be considered valid.

BR-06: The total amount of a sale must be calculated automatically based on product prices and quantities.

BR-07: The system must record all inventory movements for traceability purposes.

BR-08: Estimated profit must be calculated as the difference between the sale price and the product cost.

BR-09: The system must generate alerts when product stock reaches or falls below the defined minimum level.

BR-10: User actions related to sales and inventory changes must be associated with the responsible user.
